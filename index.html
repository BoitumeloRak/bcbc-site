About the Founder

Archbishop Mashego is the esteemed founder of Bearers of Christ Church, based in Daveyton. He was ordained as a Bishop in 2022 and, through continued dedication to the work of the Lord, obtained a qualification in Ministry in 2024. His life and leadership are a testament to unwavering faith, vision, and service to the Kingdom of God.
