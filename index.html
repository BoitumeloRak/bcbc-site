About the Founder

Archbishop Mashego is the founder of Bearers of Christ Church, with a branch located in Tsakane. He received his Certificate in Ministry in 2016, followed by a Diploma in Ministry in 2017. In 2024, he was officially ordained as a bishop, further affirming his calling and commitment to the work of the Lord. He is also in partnership with Team Impact Christian University, working together to equip and empower the next generation of Christian leaders.
